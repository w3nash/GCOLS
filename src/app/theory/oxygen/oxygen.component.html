Ferrox Paper Test for Oxygen

Ferox test is the test to ensure the presence of the oxygen on the compounds, if there is a change of color red to violet then the presence of oxygen is determined to testing compound.

Objective

The objective for this experiment is to detect the presence of Oxygen by the use of different compounds.

Learning Outcomes

  1. Students acquire the skill in understanding the Ferrox Paper Test for Oxygen detection.
  2. Students understand the test to detect the presence of Oxygen in Acetone, Benzene, Ethanol, Kerosene.
  3. Students understand the chemical reactions, distinguish positive and negative result.
  4. Students acquire the skill to perform the experiment in the online lab simulation once they understand different steps in the procedure.
